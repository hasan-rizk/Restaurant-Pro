T4F-Tartous

As one of the first restaurants on Syria famed Restaurant Row in Tartous, Stephanie Izard’s TEST4FOOD began in 2010 with a goal of serving bold, global flavors to our local community (and visitors!). Since then, our desire to treat guests like family has remained the same (as have our Green Beans) – but our seasonal flavors, cocktails, wine and locally-rooted beer are ever-changing
